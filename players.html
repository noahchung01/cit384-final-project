Players

ZEUS

Zeus was T1's prodigious top laner, known for his versatile champion pool and exceptional laning phase dominance. His ability to adapt to various team compositions and consistently outplay opponents has made him a key asset in T1’s success on both domestic and international stages. As one of the younger members of T1, he continued to prove himself as one of the premier top laners in the world. Although he is no longer a part of the team due to issues related to contracts, his legacy has been cemented as one of the brightest young talents to ever grace T1's roster.

Highlight

ONER

Oner serves as T1's intelligent and proactive jungler, known for his ability to read the map and dictate the pace of the game. His synergy with Faker enables T1 to exert strong mid-jungle control, creating openings for the side lanes to flourish. Oner’s mastery of diverse jungle champions, from aggressive playmakers like Lee Sin to tanky initiators like Sejuani, gives T1 flexibility in drafts.

Highlight

FAKER

Faker, the legendary mid-laner and face of T1, remains the cornerstone of the team with his unparalleled experience and leadership. Known as the "Unkillable Demon King," his unmatched experience, deep champion pool, and ability to adapt to evolving metas have kept him relevant at the top level for over a decade. Beyond his individual prowess, his role as a mentor to T1’s younger players, such as Zeus and Oner, highlights his contribution to the team’s enduring legacy.

Highlight

GUMAYUSI

Gumayusi is T1’s explosive AD Carry, renowned for his precision and consistent damage output in team fights. With a keen eye for positioning and a fearless playstyle, Gumayusi often takes center stage as T1’s primary late-game damage dealer. His synergy with Keria in the bot lane is a critical part of T1's early-game strategy, often establishing lane dominance and translating it into map control. As one of the most talented ADCs in the world, Gumayusi’s ability to step up in clutch moments has made him an indispensable member of the roster, capable of turning the tide of any game.

Highlight

KERIA

Keria is T1’s visionary support player, celebrated for his creativity, deep champion pool, and exceptional playmaking abilities. Nicknamed the "Legendary Genius Monster," Keria has earned this title through his innovative approach to the support role and his ability to consistently outthink and outmaneuver opponents. Known for pushing the boundaries of the meta, he frequently pioneers unconventional champion picks that often become staples of professional play. His synergy with Gumayusi is one of T1's strongest assets, enabling the duo to dominate the bot lane against even the toughest opponents. His combination of mechanical skill and game sense has solidified his reputation as one of the best supports in League of Legends history.
