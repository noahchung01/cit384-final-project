About This Website

This website pays tribute to SKT T1's League of Legends roster, celebrating their incredible journey to their second championship title together and a fifth Worlds trophy for the organization. With the same players standing together through challenges and triumphs, it honors the unwavering bond they've built and the remarkable progress they've made over the past three years.

Who Is ZOFGK?

ZOFGK is an abbreviation for T1's League of Legends 5-man roster: Zeus, Oner, Faker, Gumayusi, and Keria. A brotherhood of 5 players who stuck together despite falling short several times, they have earned recognition and support from fans all around the world and became a major icon in the scene over the past few years.
